Team E2- Crash Proof Drone

Problem Description



The main idea of this project is to build a crash-proof drone. Simply put, add the proper sensor(s), to detect when the drone is about to crash (into the ground), and then take the proper evasive maneuvers.

Create an extra CrashProof mode that can be activated (and deactivated) with a switch, and when it's activated, the drone cannot be landed. Or crashed into the ground.

For extra credit also avoid front obstacles.
